Gym Exercise Routines

Exercise 1: Push-Ups

Steps to perform Push-Ups:

  • Start in a plank position.
  • Lower your body until your chest almost touches the floor.
  • Push yourself back to the starting position.

Exercise 2: Squats

Steps to perform Squats:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower your body as if sitting in a chair.
  • Keep your back straight and return to the starting position.

Exercise 3: Plank

Steps to perform Plank:

  • Start in a push-up position, but rest on your forearms.
  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Hold the position for as long as possible.
