Cross-Training Activities

Activity 1: Boxing

Steps to perform basic boxing techniques:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Keep your hands up to protect your face.
  • Practice throwing jabs and crosses while moving.

Activity 2: Battle Ropes

Steps to perform Battle Ropes training:

  • Stand with your feet hip-width apart, holding one end of the rope in each hand.
  • Engage your core and bend slightly at the knees.
  • Wave the ropes up and down or side to side for a set duration.

Activity 3: Russian Twists

Steps to perform Russian Twists:

  • Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet flat, leaning back slightly.
  • Hold a weight or medicine ball with both hands at chest level.
  • Twist your torso to the right, bringing the weight beside your hip.
  • Return to the center, then twist to the left side.
  • Continue alternating sides for the desired number of repetitions.

Activity 4 : Box Jumps

Steps to perform Box Jumps:

  • Stand in front of a sturdy box or platform, feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower into a quarter squat, swinging your arms back.
  • Explosively jump onto the box, using your arms to propel you upward.
  • Land softly with your knees slightly bent, fully standing on the box.
  • Step back down and repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
